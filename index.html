Distance, Desire, Disclosure:

In this essay I'll- (Jokes)
When making this playlist, I wasn't sure exactly what message I wanted to convey. We both know we have the same taste so anything I would've put would be kind of redundant and disingenous. We also haven't been talking much so I don't quite know much about you, we don't have any memories either so I didn't have much to draw from.

What I do know is that we are way too far away, there is a mutual desire to connect more and I would like to introduce you to many more parts of me(Disclosure). Keeping the latter in mind, I'll have you know that I love explanations and context! The playlist is constructed in such a way that the first third highlights the distance between us, the second, expresses certain desires and general things I want to say and the last portion consists of bands and artists that I love dearly. I can't say where these sections start and end that's your business
